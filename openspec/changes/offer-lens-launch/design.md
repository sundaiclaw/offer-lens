## Context
The app should feel polished, high-stakes, and trustworthy — like a private career strategy desk rather than a generic chat tool.

## Decisions
- Use a single-page app with offer input on the left and structured decision output on the right.
- Make AI central to extracting terms, surfacing leverage, identifying risk, and drafting the negotiation message.
- Emphasize clarity, confidence, and tactical usefulness.
- Optimize for "paste the offer -> inspect leverage + risks -> send the reply".
